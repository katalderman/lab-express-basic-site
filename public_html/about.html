About Kat

Photographer | Traveler | Designer | Developer | House Music Enthusiast

  • Hire Me

I was born in Richmond, Virginia, lived in New York to get my Bachelor of Arts in Psychology, and I currently live in South Florida. My love for UX design and web development drove me to make a career shift; I have 15+ years in eCommerce, but in my most recent roles, I was more of a manager than a do-er. To me, not having a creative outlet was a huge reason why I never felt completely happy in my work.

Creativity involves breaking out of established patterns in order to look at things in a different way.

Edward de Bono

Photography has been a huge part of my life. I studied alternative processes like cyanotypes, polaroid transfers, gelatin silver prints, and more as my focus. I've transferred my technical knowledge of photography and development techniques into the digital world following the release of digital SLRs and pushed the physical boundaries of my photography by mastering Lightroom and Photoshop.

To me, photography is an art of observation. It’s about finding something interesting in an ordinary place…I’ve found it has little to do with the things you see and everything to do with the way you see them.

Elliot Erwit

I am also an avid traveler, PC gamer and Bruce Campbell is my favorite actor.
