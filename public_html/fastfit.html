Project Requirements

The National Wellness Institute wants to reimagine how people can adopt and maintain a routine that enhances their well-being.

  • Focus on a category that relates to personal well-being
  • Provide a way to track the user’s progress and push them to commit to a healthier lifestyle
  • UI should reflect a fresh, updated image

Research

User Survey

A survey was conducted to acquire information on fitness habits and usage of assisting devices like fitness apps. 44 respondents indicated the following:

  • 60% have "gymphobia"
  • 52% are sedentary
  • 44% don't have time for fitness
  • 55% don't know what to do when they work out
  • 47% want planned routines to follow in their workouts

Additionally, users indicated they are motivated through youtube videos, music, and competition with other people.

Problem Statement

Busy, unsure or unmotivated people need a way to quickly work out because they don’t have the time, expertise or motivation to do so on their own.

Planned Features

Fastfit will motivate and guide users by offering the following features:

  • Pre-generated workout routines
  • Visual and audio guides
  • Achievement earning system
  • Encouraging notifications

  • User persona for "Busy Beth"

  • User journey for "Busy Beth"
